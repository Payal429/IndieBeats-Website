Mitraz

Genre: Pop Dance

Full Name: Mitraz

Occupation: Singers

Introduction

Mitraz is an Indian pop/RnB duo formed in 2020 by Pratik and Anmol. They are based in Bangalore and have released two albums, Zehen (2021) and Love/Roses (2022). Their songs have been streamed over 100 million times on Spotify and YouTube.

Early Life and Education

The duo met in college and started making music together as a hobby. They soon realized that they had a natural chemistry and decided to pursue music as a career. They released their debut single, "Akhiyaan", in 2020, which was a sleeper hit and went on to become a chart-topper.

Musical Career

Mitraz's music is a mix of pop, RnB, and electronic music. Their lyrics are often honest and relatable, and their songs are known for their catchy melodies and smooth vocals. They have a loyal fan base in India and are quickly gaining popularity worldwide. In addition to their music, Mitraz are also known for their fashion sense. They are often seen sporting trendy outfits and accessories, and they have collaborated with several fashion brands. They are also active on social media, where they share updates about their music, fashion, and lifestyle. Mitraz are one of the most promising new acts in the Indian music scene. They have a unique sound and a strong following, and they are sure to continue to grow in popularity in the years to come.
