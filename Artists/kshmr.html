Kshmr

Genre: Electro house, progressive house, future house, future bounce, big room house, psytrance

Full Name: Niles Hollowell-Dhar

Date of Birth: 6 October 1988

Place of Birth: Berkeley, California, U.S.

Occupation: Musician, Songwriter, DJ, Record Producer

Introduction

KSHMR, whose real name is Niles Hollowell-Dhar, is a highly acclaimed music producer, DJ, and songwriter known for his contributions to electronic dance music (EDM) and his unique blend of various musical influences. He has made a significant impact on the global music scene with his innovative productions, captivating performances, and commitment to pushing the boundaries of electronic music.

Early Life and Education

KSHMR was born on October 6, 1988, in Berkeley, California, USA. His interest in music developed at a young age, and he began experimenting with music production during his teenage years. He attended the University of California, Berkeley, where he pursued a degree in political science and philosophy while simultaneously honing his skills as a music producer.

Rise to Fame

KSHMR's rise to fame in the EDM world began when he was a part of the electronic music duo, The Cataracs, known for their hit single "Like a G6." After parting ways with the duo, KSHMR embarked on a solo career and adopted his stage name, which is a combination of "Kashmir" and "KSH," representing his aim to bring a fusion of cultures and musical styles into his music.

Musical Career

KSHMR gained widespread recognition for his unique sound, which incorporates elements of EDM, Indian classical music, and cinematic influences. His debut solo single, "Megalodon," released in 2014, was a massive success and marked the beginning of his solo career. One of KSHMR's signature styles is his use of ethnic and cultural sounds, such as Indian instruments and vocal samples, to create a distinctive and immersive experience in his music. In addition to his solo releases, KSHMR has also contributed to the music of several well-known artists and producers, further solidifying his status as a sought-after collaborator in the EDM industry. On February 25, Hollowell-Dhar released the single "Lion Heart" in collaboration with Indian rapper Divine and Argentine freestyle rapper Lit Killah featuring Jeremy Oceans and frequent collaborator Karra.

Achievements

KSHMR's talent and innovation have earned him numerous accolades and recognition in the EDM community. He has consistently ranked among the top DJs in the world, as per DJ Mag's Top 100 DJs poll. His electrifying performances at major music festivals and events worldwide have garnered critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base.
