Lisa Mishra

Genre: Western Pop

Full Name: Lisa Mishra

Date of Birth: 4 February 1989

Place of Birth: Brahmapur, Odisha, India

Occupation: Singer, Songwriter and Guitartist

Introduction

Lisa Mishra is a talented American-Indian singer, composer, and songwriter who has made a notable mark in the music industry with her soulful voice and captivating performances. She is known for her ability to effortlessly blend different musical styles, creating a unique and refreshing sound that resonates with a diverse audience.

Early Life and Education

Lisa Mishra was born on February 4, 1989, in Chicago, Illinois, USA. She grew up in a family that appreciated and encouraged her interest in music. Lisa pursued her education at Northwestern University, where she completed her bachelor's degree in Communications and a master's degree in Integrated Marketing Communications. During her time at university, she continued to nurture her passion for music, often performing at local events and gatherings.

Rise to Fame

Lisa Mishra's rise to fame can be attributed to her viral cover of the Bollywood hit song "Tareefan" from the film "Veere Di Wedding." In 2018, she uploaded her rendition on YouTube, which quickly gained immense popularity, capturing the attention of audiences both in India and internationally. This breakthrough cover showcased her exceptional vocal abilities and unique musical style, propelling her into the spotlight and marking the beginning of her promising career as a singer and performer.

Musical Career

Lisa Mishra's musical journey gained significant momentum when she uploaded a cover of the hit song "Tareefan" from the Bollywood film "Veere Di Wedding" on YouTube in 2018. Her rendition went viral and garnered attention from both Indian and international audiences. This marked a turning point in her career as a singer.

She continued to create covers and original music, often fusing elements of Western and Indian musical styles. Lisa's ability to sing in multiple languages, including Hindi and English, further expanded her reach and appeal to a wide audience.

Achievements

Lisa Mishra's talent and dedication to music have earned her recognition in the industry. Her cover songs and original tracks have received millions of views on YouTube and streaming platforms. She has also performed at various music festivals, concerts, and events, further solidifying her position as a rising star in the music scene.
