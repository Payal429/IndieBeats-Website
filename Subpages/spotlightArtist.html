Welcome to the IndieBeat Artist Spotlight!

Each spotlight feature is a journey into the heart and soul of these remarkable musicians, giving you a glimpse into their musical influences, hometown vibes, and the compelling stories that fuel their artistry. Whether you're a seasoned indie music aficionado or just beginning your exploration, our Artist Spotlight is your compass in this ever-evolving sonic landscape. Join us as we celebrate the artists who challenge conventions, break boundaries, and inspire us with their unique voices. From indie rock to electronic, folk to experimental, and everything in between, our Artist Spotlight series promises to be a kaleidoscope of musical diversity.

Artist Spotlight

Darshan Raval

Darshan Raval is a popular Indian singer, composer, and songwriter. He is known for his work in different languages including Hindi, Gujarati, Punjabi, and Bengali. He is known for his soulful voice, versatile singing style, and energetic stage presence.Raval's music is a blend of pop, rock, and Indian classical music. His songs often have catchy melodies and meaningful lyrics. He sings about a variety of topics, including love, heartbreak, friendship, and social issues.

Zaeden

Zaeden is an Indian-born, Dubai-based DJ and record producer. He is known for his unique sound that blends elements of EDM, hip hop, and trap. His music is often described as energetic, danceable, and catchy.Zaeden is one of the most popular DJs and record producers in India today. Kshmr

KSHMR is an American DJ, record producer, and musician. He is known for his unique sound that blends elements of electro house, big room house, Indian classical music, and world music. His music is often described as energetic, danceable, and melodic. KSHMR began his career in 2014, and he quickly rose to prominence in the electronic dance music scene. He has released music on major labels such as Spinnin' Records, Dharma Worldwide, and Sony Music. He has also collaborated with some of the biggest names in the industry, including Tiesto, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, and Alok.
